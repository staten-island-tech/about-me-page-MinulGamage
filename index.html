A Day In Life of a Staten Island Tech Student

My name is Minul Gamage. I am a sophmore at Staten Island Tech. I am currently making this about me page in my AP computer science class. My average day at Tech starts at 6 a.m when I wake up. I eat breakfast and get to school at 7:50. I first go to English and after that I go to my Phys ed class. Then I go to AP computer science, physics, algebra, CAD, then finally lunch. After lunch I go to AP world history and lastly Russian. After school, I will sometimes hangout and play basketball with my friends. At about 5 I arrive home and study for about an hour. The activities I do after I study vary but I will usually play video games, draw, or read. Finally, I go to sleep at 10:30. That's about it.
